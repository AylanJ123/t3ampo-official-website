Our history!

T3AMPO was founded on May 21, 2021, as a joint initiative and interdisciplinary indie game developer with the goal of creating rich interactive experiencies.

Our name "T3AMPO" arises from the combination of the words "Team", the musical term "Tempo", and "3" for the number of our core values: Informed, Inclusive, and Inspired.

We aim on exploring, encourage and facilitate cooperation dynamics, competence and user learning through innovative, educational and entertaining experiences. To achieve our goal, we offer interactive media with high quality art, sound and player feedback carefully crafted by a team of young, talented and interdisciplinary team.

We have got more than two years of experience in video game design and production and a portfolio of 7 prototypes, pitchdeck presentation, participation in important videogame development ecosystem events, business fairs and even international competitions.
