GroabiaTimes

Crafted for Good Old Game Jam CIDEV, 2022

Theme: We are living in dystopia

How good is the truth? Your duty is to inform people, and keep your job…

The player makes choices. Good and bad outcomes are circumstantial. Point and click at certain locations in the screen and take photos to tell your stories, or disrupt the scene making it more dramatic.

Each day you work for the most famous newspaper in the country: Groabia Times. Your boss wants you to tell the "truth" they need, but the delicate state of this government makes it quite hard. Try not to anger your superiors or the townfolk, it won't end good.

On each scene you can ask your team to help you dumper the visual evidence, in order to make people look like rebels, or to help them and show how corrupt this dictatorship is. Survive and feed your family!
