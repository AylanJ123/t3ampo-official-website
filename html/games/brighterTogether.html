Brighter Toghether

3rd place Winner at GameJam CiDev 2021

Theme: Stay Connected

Feed the flames!

Brighter Together is a cooperative platformer in the dark! Players must explore the room for fuel and resources in order to to stay alive and advance in the game.

The Fire Siblings have different skills. While the blue one can glide and jump high, it will burn boxes on touch. But the orange one is heavier, so it can press buttons and jump and move boxes.

Fire up the torches to move platforms and get access to new areas, or find some spilt oil to burn stronger for a short time. Watch out! The Siblings are weak, if they separate too much or their line of sight gets cut, they might die out! Stay together and do the puzzles.
