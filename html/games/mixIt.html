Mix it

Best Sound Design Award , Top 5 LATAM, Anjo Tintas Category, Best Game of CR at GJ+ First stage.

Mix and unmix!

Mix it! is an innovative tool that can help players experience, understand, and learn about color. Challenging and colorful Endless Runner, single-player space adventure at the edge of a collapsing blackhole.

Players explore how colors interact and combine them in order to advance in the game. There exist 8 different colors Each one with their own shape and color.

In Mix It!, there are 8 colors. You start as with a white Ship, but you will find Painters in your space travel. They will combine your current color with their own, this might mix secondaries, primaries, white or black. Turning white will damage your ship and turning black will lure beasts into eating you.

Watch out for The Blob! A void-born creature that loves eating color, after eating for a while it can spit Painters at enemies. If you turn black The Blob will advance towards you until it eats you, don't let it happen!
